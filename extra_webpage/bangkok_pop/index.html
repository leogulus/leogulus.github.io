Changes in Bangkok Population from 2009 to 2016

The first seven panels below show the difference in the population in each khwang in Bangkok from 2009 (2552) to 2016 (2559). The last two panels show the population density (person per square kilometer) in 2010 and 2015 as the base reference for the crowdedness in Bangkok.

Reference: The shapefile for khwang in Bangkok comes from Bangkok GIS and the information about population is obtained from Offical Statistics Registration Systems. (The information will be in Thai). The template for this webpage is from Freebie: 4 Bootstrap Gallery Templates.
