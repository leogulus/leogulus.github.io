Author: Taweewat Somboonpanyakul

First Plots
These first plots are the data from candidates who applied in 2015 with writing GRE score as the color of the first plot
and aggregated in the second plot.

Second Plots
Blue dots represent candidates who applied to Physics PhD program in 2015 while red dots represent those who applied in 2014.
The dataset is by no means representing the true sample of all Physics PhD candidates, but it give us a first look into any possible correlation between Physics GRE scores and the other two criterions.
